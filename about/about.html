I am a panoramic photographer and application software developer based in my home town of San Antonio, Texas.

I started my career over 30 years ago writing COBOL programs using punched cards on a Sperry Univac 90/30. Since then I have written a variety of applications across different hardware and software platforms up to Javascript/CSS/HTML and the mobile web applications of today. A couple of years ago I took up photography and have been shooting high quality stock and panoramic images around the world. I enjoy the challenge of composing an image in a full 360 degrees and the interactive results that can be shared online.
